What if I miss a class?
No problem at all! We offer flexible rescheduling with 24 hours notice. Missed sessions are never forfeited — they're simply rescheduled at a time that works for you, with no extra charges.
Do you help with the learning license?
Absolutely. Our License Assistance package covers both the Learning License and Permanent License applications. We prepare all documents, guide you through the online RTO portal, and accompany you on test day if needed.
How far does the pickup service go?
We cover all of Shivane, Warje, Kothrud, Bavdhan, and surrounding areas within 8km of our base. Contact us and we'll confirm if your location is within our pickup zone — we're expanding constantly.
What kind of car will I learn on?
We use well-maintained modern 4-wheelers with dual controls (instructor-side brake/clutch) for maximum safety. Both manual and automatic transmission options are available based on your preference and licensing goal.
How long does it take to get a Permanent License?
Typically 25–35 days total: 7 days to get the Learning License, mandatory 30-day waiting period by RTO rules, then the Permanent License test. We guide you through every step and keep you informed throughout.
